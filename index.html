Report approach

Prepared with inspection, documentation, and market evidence.

A professional valuation is more than a rate calculation. It requires verification of property details, review of available records, consideration of development potential, comparison with market evidence, and clear reporting suitable for the stated purpose.

01 Document review

Ownership papers, plans, tax records, allotment details, and available approvals.

02 Physical inspection

Area, frontage, access, construction condition, age, occupancy, and site features.

03 Market analysis

Comparable rates, location demand, utility, saleability, and present market trend.
